Hon.-Prof. Dr.-Ing. Martin Otter ‒ Short Biography

 

Career

since 1.1.2025 Project leader at DLR Institute of Vehicle Concepts
2023 - 2024 Acting director of DLR Institute of System Dynamics and Control
2014 - 2023 Vice director of DLR Institute of System Dynamics and Control
2007 - 2014 Senior scientist at DLR Oberpfaffenhofen
1984 - 2007 Researcher at DLR Oberpfaffenhofen

Other Positions   

since 2024 Board member for Technology at the International Modelica Association
since 2004 Honorary professor at Technical University of Munich
2000 - 2024 Chair of the International Modelica Association
since 1997 Lecturer at Technical University of Munich (course description 2025)

Scientific education

1994 Dissertation (Dr.-Ing.) at Ruhruniversität Bochum (summa cum laude)
1984 Degree in mechanical engineering (Dipl.-Ing.) at Technical University of Munich

Activities

  • Development of the Web-Apps Modiator (Modelica Instant Simulation) and Modicalc (Modelica Instant Calculator) together with Hilding Elmqvist.
  • 24 dissertations supervised and/or reviewed.
  • About 170 publications (elib DLR, google scholar profile)
  • Co-organiser (program/editoral board) of 15 International Modelica Conferences.
    Local organizer of the Modelica Conferences in 2002 in Oberpfaffenhofen and in 2012 in Munich.
  • Co-organizer of ITEA projects EUROSYSLIB,  MODELISAR,  MODRIO,  EMPHYSIS,  OpenSCALING
    (total budget of these projects: 85 Mill. €).
  • Project leader of the Functional Mock-up Interface (FMI) for Model Exchange standard in 2008-2012
    (FMI is supported by > 240 tools)
  • Project leader of the Modelica Association Project "Modelica Language" in 2000 - 2016
    (Modelica is supported by > 10 tools).
  • Project leader for the development of the Modelica Standard Library in 2000 - 2012.
